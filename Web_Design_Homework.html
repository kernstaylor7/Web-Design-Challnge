Airplane Crashes Through the Years

The purpose of this analysis is to gain a deeper understanding of how air travel safety has changed through the decades. Particularly we analyzed three spans of time: 1915-1925, 1966-1975, and 2005-2015. In these three timeframes we took a deeper look into crash per year, top three plane types most commonly crashed, and mortality rate.
