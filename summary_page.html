Summary of Findings

In conclusion, there is not definite answer to if air travel is safer. It does appear to be off the cuff, but there were many contributing factors not looked into and many years not analyzed. It is widely known that air travel is one of the safety modes of transportation one could take, but at what rate safety has increased or decreased is up to the passengers to decide.
